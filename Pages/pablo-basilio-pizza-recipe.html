Pablo Basilio Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 550-580 [g] nimeral or filtered water
  • 3 [g] yeast
  • 22-28 [g] salt
  • 1000 [g] flour 0000
  • you can replace some part of the flour with whole wheat flour

Process

Combine the ingredients

The water need to be cold, not freeze but cold, then we dissolve the yest on the water. Now we can start to slowly incorporate the flour, when you have added half of the flour, add the salt. knead until all the ingredients are incorporated

block fermentation

Cover the dough with a bowl an let the magic of fermentation succses for 2-3 hours.

Cut the buns

Divide the dough into 4 or 5 equal parts, then you need to give form to the buns with te claw or joystick technique. Let the buns rest at ambiental temperature for 30[min].

Final fermentation

After the rest at ambiental temperature let the buns in the freezer all the night and get them out 3[h] before you gona cook the pizza.
