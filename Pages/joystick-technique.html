Joystick technique

  1. Grip the dough like a joystick
  2. Use your index, middle, ring and pinky for put the border of the dough in the center of the bottom part of the dough
  3. after you close the union you form in the center of the bottom of the dough you gonna have a oval shape, now fold the oval in their short axis.
  4. Close the last union we form and now use your two hand to make a sphere.
  5. is important that the dough is "close" in the bottom once we finish
